### Decisions made

- **EndorseKit gets a Go backend.** It has server-side
  endorsement-PDF generation, a daily booking/lesson reminder cron, and
  Stripe Connect invoicing — all of which the founder's backend rule
  assigns to a Go service on Cloud Run. The web tier stays SvelteKit.
  Formal call recorded in ADR-0001 (proposed). No Python service — there
  is no ML component in V1.
- **The endorsement registry is append-only** (ADR-0002) — the
  legal-record heart of the product. An issued endorsement row is never
  edited or deleted; corrections are new superseding records; every row
  is HMAC-sealed and hash-chained to the prior record.
- **E-signature approach** (ADR-0003, proposed): typed-name + an
  explicit intent affirmation bound to the sealed content hash and the
  authenticated CFI identity. A drawn-signature image is V1.1.
- **Stripe Connect for CFI→student invoicing** (ADR-0004) — the CFI is
  paid through their own connected account; EndorseKit is the platform,
  never merchant of record for a lesson. The CFI's own $12/mo
  subscription runs on the platform account (standard Checkout).
- **Billing model** (ADR-0005): a flat $12/mo (or $120/yr) subscription
  with NO `application_fee` on the Connect path — the CFI keeps 100% of
  student payments minus Stripe's processing fee.
- Three product-specific auditor subagents created alongside
  `spec-guardian`: `endorsement-registry-auditor`,
  `stripe-connect-auditor`, `rls-and-tenancy-auditor`.
